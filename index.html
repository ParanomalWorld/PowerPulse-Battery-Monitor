1. Information We Collect

PowerPulse does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from users. Our app is designed to provide battery monitoring services directly on your device, with all data remaining securely within the app environment.

To enhance user experience, PowerPulse may collect limited non-personal data, such as anonymized usage statistics, which helps us improve app performance and functionality. This data does not include any personal information and is used solely for analytical purposes.

PowerPulse accesses battery data from your device to display real-time battery status, usage history, charging status, and battery health metrics. This information is processed locally within the app and is not transmitted outside of your device.
